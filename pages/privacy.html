Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Introduction

ALE International ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of users of the Rainbow platform and this website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store and protect your personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable French data protection laws.

Data Controller

The data controller is ALE International, 32 avenue Kléber, 92700 Colombes, France. For any questions regarding your personal data, you may contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@al-enterprise.com.

Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Account information: name, email address, company name, phone number and password when you create an account.
  • Payment information: billing address and payment card details (processed securely by Stripe; we do not store full card numbers).
  • Usage data: pages visited, features used, device information and IP address for analytics and service improvement.
  • Communications: messages sent through our contact form and support requests.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide, maintain and improve the Rainbow platform and related services.
  • To manage your account, process subscriptions and handle payments.
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.
  • To ensure the security and integrity of our systems.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms of service.

Legal Basis

We process your personal data based on: (a) the performance of our contract with you, (b) your consent where applicable, (c) our legitimate business interests, and (d) compliance with legal obligations.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with: (a) service providers who process data on our behalf (Stripe for payments, Brevo for transactional emails, Railway for hosting), (b) legal authorities when required by law, and (c) business partners only with your explicit consent.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Account data is deleted within 30 days of account closure, unless retention is required by law. Payment records are retained for the legally required period (typically 10 years in France for accounting purposes).

Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Access: request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure: request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Restriction: request limited processing of your data.
  • Portability: receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection: object to processing based on legitimate interests.

To exercise your rights, contact us at dpo@al-enterprise.com. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the CNIL (Commission Nationale de l'Informatique et des Libertés).

Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data, including encryption in transit (TLS), secure password hashing, access controls and regular security audits.

Cookies

This website uses essential cookies for language preferences and authentication. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. Essential cookies are necessary for the website to function and do not require consent under GDPR.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact

For any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at dpo@al-enterprise.com or write to: ALE International, Data Protection Officer, 32 avenue Kléber, 92700 Colombes, France.
