About Us

Connecting the World Through Innovation

Alcatel-Lucent Enterprise delivers secure, reliable and innovative communication solutions to over 830,000 customers worldwide.

Our Story

Alcatel-Lucent Enterprise (ALE) is a leading provider of communications solutions and services, with a heritage spanning over a century of engineering excellence. Headquartered in France, ALE serves businesses, governments and institutions in more than 100 countries.

Rainbow, our flagship cloud communication platform, was created with a clear mission: to make enterprise collaboration simple, secure and accessible for organizations of every size. Combining messaging, video conferencing, cloud telephony and business integrations, Rainbow is the bridge between traditional infrastructure and the digital workplace.

Today, ALE employs over 2,300 people worldwide and partners with a network of 3,100+ business partners to deliver solutions tailored to each customer's unique needs.
